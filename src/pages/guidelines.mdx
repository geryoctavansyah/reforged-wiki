# Community Guidelines

Welcome to the community guidelines for our Minecraft server wiki! Here you will find how to use the wiki effectively and how you can contribute to making it better.

## How to Use the Wiki

### Navigation

- Use the sidebar or the search bar to find articles.
- Explore categories to find related topics easily.

### Reading Articles

- Read through articles thoroughly.
- Use the table of contents for longer articles to jump to relevant sections.

## How to Contribute to the Wiki

### Creating an Account

Sign up for a Github account to start contributing, and verify your email address if required.

### Editing Pages

Click the "Edit this page" in right sidebar on any page to start editing. Use the following markdown formatting guide to help you structure your edits:

```
# Header 1
## Header 2
### Header 3
**Bold Text**
*Italic Text*
- Bullet Point
- Another Bullet Point
1. Numbered List
2. Second Item
[Link Text](https://example.com)
![Image Alt Text](https://example.com/image.jpg)
```

### Community Contributions

- Follow the same guidelines and rules as in-game conduct.
- Be respectful and constructive in your edits and comments.
- Use proper citations and give credit where it is due.

### Review and Approval

- Major edits and new pages may require review by a moderator before being published.
- Regular contributors can apply for more advanced editing permissions.
